Our Beginnings

Brandeburg Development Group, Inc., Real Estate Brokerage Office was opened in August 2003. We specialize in the sale of commercial and investment property and vacant land for commercial developments or large tracts of land 500 - 5,000 acres for major housing developments. We are also the Leasing Agent for over 100,000 sf of upscale office space in Leesburg and Industrial/Commercial-retail space in Lake County. We work closely with regional planning agencies, county governments and local governments throughout central Florida. Involved in issues of planning, zoning, annexation and to better understand where staff support is for the viability of certain projects or changes in the use of land or existing improvements on property. We have participated in development with use of Small Cities Block Grants and with planning for Community Development Districts (CDD's). 2004's revenue on real estate was about $13 million with another $1.2 million in leasing sales. The first half of 2005 we have had sales of $10.5 million. Total sales of 2005 is expected to range between $21 million to $30 million. We are an active brokerage office.
